Hi! I am Kavya Karumuri, a freshman studying Neurobiology. During my free time,
I love listening to music, I (try to) play my guitar, I love watching movies, and reading for fun!

I love being a woman in STEM, but the load of assignments and exams always has me stressed and leaves me super tired by the end of the week.
When I first moved to college, freshman me had a hard time taking time out for self-care every day. But slowly I realized that I was doing a lot
for myself and taking time out for myself for an hour a day at least is very important. So, I incorporated different self-care activities
into my everyday routines. Since then, I do not burn out, my mood is so much better throughout the day. I really hope this helps you too!
